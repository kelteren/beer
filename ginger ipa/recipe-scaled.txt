Linearly scaled to half of the original volume.

Ingredients:
  170 g American Crystal 20L
  115 g American Crystal 60L
   60 g Belgian Biscuit
  1.5 kg light LME
  225 g Honey
   30 g Galena hops (12.2%)
    5 g Chinook hops (11.6%)
   30 g Fuggles hops (4.6%)
   30 g Ginger
   15 g Amarillo hops (optional for dry hopping)
   1 vial White Labs California Ale yeast
 .37 cups corn sugar
 .37 cups water

OG: 1.055
FG: 1.013
ABV: 5.5%

Steep the grains for 30 minutes in 6 liters at 66 C. Gradually
bring temperature up to 77 C.

Add 1.5kg LME and 225g honey. Bring to boil. Start timer.

Schedule:
  0  minutes: 15 g Galena hops
  15 minutes: 15 g Galena hops
  30 minutes:  5 g Chinook hops
  45 minutes:  5 g Fuggles hops
              30 g chopped ginger
  60 minutes: 25 g Fuggles (flameout)

The ginger should be kinda finely chopped. But not too finely. Aim for
"lagom".

Top off to 10.5l. Cool to room temperature. Add yeast. Ferment.

A reader comment suggest dry hopping with 15 g Amarillo hops.
