Source: http://tapirtantrum.com/2009/01/03/ginger-ipa/

Ingredients:
  340 g American Crystal 20L
  230 g American Crystal 60L
  115 g Belgian Biscuit
   3 kg light LME
  450 g Honey
   60 g Galena hops (12.2%)
   10 g Chinook hops (11.6%)
   60 g Fuggles hops (4.6%)
   60 g Ginger
   30 g Amarillo hops (optional for dry hopping)
   1 vial White Labs California Ale yeast
 .75 cups corn sugar
 .75 cups water

OG: 1.055
FG: 1.013
ABV: 5.5%

Steep the grains for 30 minutes in 11.4 liters at 66 C. Gradually
bring temperature up to 77 C.

Add 3kg LME and 450g honey. Bring to boil. Start timer.

Schedule:
  0  minutes: 30 g Galena hops
  15 minutes: 30 g Galena hops
  30 minutes: 10 g Chinook hops
  45 minutes: 15 g Fuggles hops
              60 g chopped ginger
  60 minutes: 45 g Fuggles (flameout)

The ginger should be kinda finely chopped. But not too finely. Aim for
"lagom".

Top off to 21l. Cool to room temperature. Add yeast. Ferment.

A reader comment suggest dry hopping with 30 g Amarillo hops.
